    What to do while there

    1. EAT!

      2. Seriously, Thailand has some of the best and cheapest food in the World.
      In the US eating nine meals a day would give you type two diabetes.
      Luckily Thai food is also some of the healthiest food in the world.


      The first time I went to thailand to meet Junes' Family,
      I thought they were trying to kill me.
      every time we walked by a food stall, vendor or restaurant
      either her, or my mother or sister in law would shove a plate of tasty looking food into my face.
      by five o'clock every day I just wanted nothing more to do with food ever again.
